Assign Your Proxy

What is a proxy? A proxy lets someone you trust vote on your behalf at HOA meetings when you can't attend. Your chosen delegate will cast your vote. You can revoke this proxy at any time before the meeting from your proxies page.
Directed: Your delegate must follow your voting instructions. Undirected: Your delegate votes at their discretion.
Specify how your delegate should vote on known items. For any item not listed, your delegate will vote at their discretion.
