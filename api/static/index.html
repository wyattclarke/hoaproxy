HOAproxy

Find your HOA. Read the rules. Vote without showing up.

HOAproxy is an independent tool for homeowners — not your board, not your management company. Upload your HOA's governing documents and let anyone in your community search them, assign proxy votes, and propose changes.

Transparent rules

Searchable documents, with a chatbot. Upload your CC&Rs, bylaws, and rules — then ask questions in plain English.

Online voting (by proxy)

Assign your vote to a trusted neighbor by e‑signing a proxy assignment form right here. They print it and bring it to the next meeting.

Community proposals

Serious proposals from real neighbors. Three co-signers required. Confirmed residents only. No comments. No gossip.
